About This App

Welcome to the Offline Notepad App!

Our app empowers you to take notes and stay organized, even when you're offline.

App Features:

  • Create and save notes effortlessly, which are stored securely in your browser in local storage.
  • Install our app on supported browsers and access your notes offline anytime. Learn how to install as a PWA
  • Enjoy a comfortable reading experience with our Dark Mode.
  • Rest easy knowing we prioritize your privacy - we never collect your data.
  • Experience lightning-fast performance - our app loads almost instantly.
  • We believe in open-source - you can contribute to our project on GitHub.

Important: Remember to back up your notes regularly since they are stored locally in your browser.
